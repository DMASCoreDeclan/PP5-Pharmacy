History

Phelans pharmacy has been in existence for almost 100 years. Originally founded by Sylvie Phelan, the business was then taken over by Dick Phelan and years later Dick was succeeded by his son Richard. The business was subsequently taken over in July 2016 by Brian Tynan. Many people will be familiar with Brian from his former fortnightly pharmacy slot on the Deise AM show on WLR.

PSI Registration Number: 7676

Our Ethos

In Phelan’s Pharmacy healthcare is personal. Phelan’s staff are renowned for attending patients’ every need with confidence, understanding and empathy. Central to this is our ability to carefully listen and understand, thereby providing the best care and attention.
